Seascape accommodation

A light-filled home overlooking Polzeath.

Three bedrooms, three bathrooms and generous living space for up to seven guests.

The house

Comfortable, practical and close to everything.

Built during the winter of 2007/08, Seascape combines modern open-plan living with a flexible bedroom layout and clear views towards the sea.

The ground floor centres on a well-equipped kitchen, dining area and large lounge. Bi-fold doors open onto the decked terrace, creating an easy indoor–outdoor space for sunny meals and evening views.

Two bedrooms are on the ground floor. The first-floor main bedroom has sea views, a Juliet balcony and its own shower room. Bedrooms include fitted storage, and beds can be made up to suit your arrival instructions.

Living spaces

Set up for relaxed self-catering.

The kitchen includes an electric oven, gas hob, microwave, air fryer, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, toaster and kettle. A separate utility area provides a washing machine, tumble dryer and drinks fridge.

The dining table seats eight, with a high chair available. The lounge opens directly onto the terrace, where outdoor furniture and a charcoal barbecue make the most of clear evenings.
