Polzeath & North Cornwall

Make the coast your itinerary.

Surfing, swimming, rock pooling, walking and golf—all within easy reach of Seascape.

Polzeath Beach

From breakfast to sunset, stay close to the sea.

The beach is ideal for surfing, swimming and rock pooling. The village also provides shops, cafés and restaurants within a few minutes’ walk of the house.

Beach

Surf, swim and rock pool

Polzeath’s broad beach is known for surf and family beach days. Surfs Up Surf School offers tuition from beginner to advanced level.

Walking

Follow the coast path

Walk the rugged north coast between Port Isaac and Polzeath, or take the estuary route towards Rock.

Golf

Play renowned courses

Several challenging courses are nearby, including St Enodoc and Trevose.

A base for North Cornwall

Stay local or explore further.

Seascape’s location makes it easy to enjoy Polzeath without driving, while nearby coastal villages and countryside offer plenty for longer days out.

Conditions at the coast can change quickly. Check tide times, weather, lifeguard information and local safety guidance before swimming, surfing or walking exposed sections of coast.
