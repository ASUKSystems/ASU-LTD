Privacy

Privacy information.

How information is handled when you contact Seascape Holidays through this website.

Last updated: 19 July 2026

About this website

This website provides information about Seascape Holidays and the Seascape property in Polzeath. The website does not use analytics, advertising cookies, user accounts or an online payment system.

Information you provide

When you call or email, you may provide information such as your name, contact details, preferred dates, number of guests and booking requirements. This information is used to respond to your enquiry, administer a booking and communicate about your stay.

The enquiry form

The contact form on this website does not transmit information to a website database. It prepares an email in your own email application. The information is only sent after you choose to send that email.

Third-party websites

Links may take you to the current Simply Owners property listing or a mapping service. Those websites operate under their own privacy and cookie policies. Review their information before supplying personal information.

Retention and sharing

Enquiry and booking correspondence may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to manage the booking, address questions, maintain financial records and meet legal obligations. Information is not sold. It may be shared where necessary to administer a stay, process a payment, comply with law or obtain professional advice.

Your choices

You can ask what personal information is held about you, request correction of inaccurate information, or raise a privacy concern by emailing info@seascapeholidays.net.
