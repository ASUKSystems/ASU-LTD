Booking information

Important information before booking.

A clear summary of how enquiries and bookings for Seascape are handled.

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Enquiries and availability

Website information and availability indications are provided as a guide. Please contact the owners to confirm current availability, price, facilities and any requirement that is important to your stay.

Booking confirmation

Sending an enquiry does not create a booking or reserve dates. A booking is confirmed only when the owners have accepted it, supplied the applicable booking information and received any payment required to secure the stay.

Prices and payments

The current published guide price is £756 to £2,947 per week, with a minimum stay of seven nights. The final price, payment schedule, accepted payment method and any security or damage deposit will be confirmed directly by the owners before you commit.

What is included

Bed linen is included. Towels may be supplied for an additional charge. Travel cot and high chair availability should be confirmed when booking. Do not assume that a facility or service is included unless it has been confirmed for your stay.

Guest responsibilities

Guests are expected to use the property responsibly, respect the stated occupancy limit, avoid nuisance to neighbours, follow safety information and leave the property in a reasonable condition. The property is non-smoking.

Changes and cancellation

The cancellation, amendment and refund arrangements applying to a booking will be supplied by the owners as part of the booking process. Read and retain those terms before making payment.

Accuracy

Reasonable care is taken to keep this website accurate, but property details, prices and availability can change. The owner’s written booking confirmation takes precedence over general website content.

Questions

For clarification before booking, email info@seascapeholidays.net or call +44 (0)1208 863638.
